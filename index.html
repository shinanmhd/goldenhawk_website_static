Our Flagship Venture: Eco-Friendly Manufacturing

Golden Hawk Enterprises is proud to lead the charge against single-use plastics through our state-of-the-art non-woven bag factory, located right here in the Maldives. We provide a premium, reusable alternative that helps protect our pristine marine ecosystems.

Sustainable Solutions

Our primary focus is on reducing plastic pollution. Our durable, reusable non-woven bags are the ideal solution for resorts, retailers, and communities looking to eliminate single-use plastic bags and protect our oceans.

Local Production & Economy

By manufacturing locally, we support the Maldivian economy, create valuable jobs, and reduce the carbon footprint associated with importing goods. This ensures a reliable supply chain for our partners across the atolls.

Custom Branding

We offer high-quality, custom printing services, allowing businesses to transform a simple bag into a powerful branding tool. Showcase your commitment to sustainability with bags featuring your logo and design.

Quality & Durability

Built to last, our non-woven bags are designed for repeated use. They are strong, water-resistant, and stylish, offering your customers a superior and dependable alternative to single-use plastics.

About Golden Hawk

Golden Hawk Enterprises is more than a holding company; we are a catalyst for progress in the Maldives. We believe that the path to a prosperous future lies in strategic investment, local innovation, and a profound respect for our fragile environment. Our approach is to identify key challenges within the Maldivian economy and build businesses that offer effective, sustainable solutions.

  • Expert Team: Our certified professionals bring decades of industry experience.
  • Innovative Approach: We leverage cutting-edge technology to solve complex challenges.
  • Client-Centric: Your success is our primary metric. We're dedicated to your goals.

We are founded on a deep-rooted commitment to our nation's future, combining international business standards with an intimate understanding of the local landscape.

Our Mission

To identify, develop, and manage businesses that provide practical solutions to local challenges, create meaningful employment for Maldivians, and contribute to a resilient and circular economy.

Our Vision

A Maldives where economic prosperity and environmental stewardship go hand in hand, creating a legacy of opportunity and natural beauty for generations to come.
